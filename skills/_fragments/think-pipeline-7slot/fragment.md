## 7-SLOT FRAME (imported: think-pipeline-7slot)

Fill every slot before designing a mechanism. An empty slot is an ungrounded design.

1. **Actor** — who acts.
2. **Trigger** — what starts it.
3. **Precondition** — what must hold first.
4. **Mechanism** — the move itself.
5. **Artifact** — what it produces.
6. **Acceptance** — how you know it worked (machine-checkable: a command, path, or comparison).
7. **Ripple** — what becomes possible next.

Artifact: a filled 7-slot table. Acceptance: no slot is empty and slot 6 is machine-shaped.
