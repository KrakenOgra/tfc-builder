## GROUND (imported: realthink-ground)

Before amplifying any claim, ground it. List each load-bearing claim, then tag it:

- **[grounded]** — backed by evidence you can point to (a file, a command output, a quote).
- **[assumed]** — believed but unverified. Name the check that would verify it.
- **[unknown]** — you do not know. Stop and find out before building on it.

**Gate:** if the CRUX of the work rests on an `[assumed]` or `[unknown]` claim, HALT and
ground it first. A beautiful plan on an ungrounded crux is rejected.

Artifact: a claims-ledger with every claim tagged. Acceptance: the crux claim is `[grounded]`.
