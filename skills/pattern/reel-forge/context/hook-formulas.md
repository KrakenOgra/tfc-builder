## Hook Formulas
source: reel-forge/SKILL.md

**H1. [Common belief] is wrong. (CONTRARIAN)**
Trigger: Topic has a dominant false assumption the audience holds.
Example: "Posting every day is ruining your growth."
Rule: Must name the belief before the refutation. Do not open with "Most people think…" — open with the belief itself.

**H2. [Specific result] in [specific time]. Here's how. (HORMOZI)**
Trigger: Topic is a method with a measurable outcome.
Example: "47 clients. 90 days. No ads. Here's the exact DM sequence."
Rule: Result and time must be real and specific. Fabricating a number to fit the HOOK violates the honesty gate — emit NEEDS_CONTEXT instead.

**H3. I [specific failure] so you don't have to. (BUILDER)**
Trigger: Creator has a firsthand mistake with a named consequence.
Example: "I spent $12,000 on a course that taught me nothing. Here's what actually worked."
Rule: The failure must be specific (dollar amount, tool name, time period). Generic "I made mistakes" does not open the curiosity gap.

**H4. [Number] things I wish I knew before [specific event]. (BUILDER fast-lane)**
Trigger: Topic has a clear before/after the creator has lived.
Example: "3 things I wish I knew before quitting my job."
Rule: Number must be ≤ 3 for a 60s Reel. More than 3 items cannot be paid off in time — the open loop stays open and the viewer exits frustrated.

**H5. The thing nobody tells you about [topic]. (CONTRARIAN soft)**
Trigger: Topic has a hidden mechanism most practitioners skip.
Example: "The thing nobody tells you about going viral."
Rule: The SCRIPT must reveal an actual mechanism, not a generic truth. "Post consistently" after this HOOK = pattern-break followed by disappointment.

**H6. [Aphorism that reframes the topic]. (NAVAL)**
Trigger: Topic is a timeless principle, not a tactic.
Example: "Earn with your mind, not your time."
Rule: First line is the full HOOK. No setup, no "in this video." The aphorism must be complete and saveable as a standalone sentence. CTA: none.
